Description

Congee is a porridge made by cooking rice in more water than usual for longer than usual. It's a very comforting and nutritious dish, perfect for weekly meal prep and ripe for a variety of toppings.

This version starts with breaking down a whole chicken to make chicken broth and incorporating the chicken meat as a source of protein. Suggested toppings are listed in the 'Tips and Tricks' section.
