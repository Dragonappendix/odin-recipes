Pork Gyoza

Home


Description

Gyoza are Japanese potstickers, typically filled with ground meat and vegetables. This version uses ground pork, green cabbage, and green onion.



Ingredients

  • 500g ground pork
  • 1 bunch green onions, diced
  • 1 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp ginger
  • 50g cabbage, diced
  • 1 pack dumpling wrappers

Instructions

Broth

  1. Break down chicken by removing breasts, thighs, drumsticks, and wings.
  2. Debone dark meat and place carcass, drumstick meat, and wings in Instant Pot.
  3. Add water to cover bones and pressure cook on high pressure for 2 hours, using natural pressure release.
  4. Use a slotted spoon to remove bones, picking off big pieces of meat.

Congee

  1. Add breasts and thighs to broth and simmer for 15 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through.
  2. Remove and shred meat. Add back to pot along with remaining meat and rice.
  3. Cook for 15-20 minutes on Soup/Broth setting or until congee is at desired consistency.
  4. Garnish as desired and enjoy!

Tips and Tricks

  • The toppings listed all work as toppings, but add whatever you like! Mix and match to change things up.
  • Congee stores well in the fridge for 5 days or in the freezer for 3 months. Reheat in the microwave until heated through.
