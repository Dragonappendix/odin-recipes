Gyoza

Home


Description

Gyoza are Japanese potstickers, typically filled with ground meat and vegetables. This version uses ground pork, green cabbage, and green onion.



Ingredients

  • 500g ground pork
  • 1 bunch green onions, diced
  • 1 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp ginger
  • 50g cabbage, diced
  • 1 pack dumpling wrappers


Instructions

Preparing Filling

  1. Combine ground pork, green cabbage, and green onion in a large bowl.
  2. Add garlic and ginger to meat mixture and combine.
  3. Add soy sauce, sesame oil, and (optionally) cornstarch to filling and combine.

Filling Gyoza

  1. Lay one dumpling wrapper in your hand and add one spoonful of filling to the center of the wrapper.
  2. Brush the edges of the wrapper with water using your finger and fold the wrapper over the filling.
  3. Create any additional folds or design to your dumpling.
  4. Freeze extra dumplings or enjoy fresh!

Cooking Gyoza

  1. For boiled gyoza:
    1. Add dumplings to a pot of boiling water and let cook for 6-7 minutes.
  2. For fried gyoza:
    1. Add dumplings to a cold pan with oil and water.
    2. Turn heat to medium high and cover the pan to let the gyoza steam.
    3. Uncover when you hear the gyoza frying in oil (the water has evaporated).
    4. Cook until the bottom of the dumplings are golden brown.
  3. Enjoy your gyoza plain or with a mix of soy sauce, sesame oil, and chili oil!
